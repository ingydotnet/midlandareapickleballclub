​​​​​​​​TAY SHORES PUBLIC SCHOOL
125 Jephson St., Victoria Harbour, ON, L0K2A0


There are 2 indoor courts at the school.

The gym floor is some kind of rubberized material and is gentle on the knees.
Great lighting.
Good run out space behind each court.
Gym is divided by a curtain, which hangs an inch above the floor.
They are the two best indoor courts in the Tay Township area and beyond.




DAYS TO PLAY AT TAY:


Mondays - WOMEN's NIGHT:
Playing from 6:00 to 9:30 PM.


Tuesdays - MEN's NIGHT:
Competitive players: 6:00 to 7:45 PM
Recreational players: 7:45 to 9:30 PM


Wednesdays - MIXED NIGHT:
Competitive players: 6:00 to 9:30 PM


Thursdays - MIXED NIGHT:
Recreational players: 6:00 to 9:30 PM


We welcome men and women on either Wednesday or Thursday nights,pick one, not both.

Please choose, which MIXED night you like to play and refrain from showing up on both nights.
As there are only two courts, it would ease long wait times for a free court on each night.

The executive of the club does not want to get into the business of telling people where they can and can’t play.
You can self select which night you wish to play, but please try to be respectful of the concept that more advanced/competitive players should have the opportunity to play with similarly skilled players, same goes for recreational players.

On the other hand, advanced and more competitive players should respect the fact that the club as a whole benefits when more skilled players also play with people who might not be at their level yet as it helps all players develop their skills.

HOW TO ENJOY PLAYING IN OUR CLUB AT TAY SHORES PUBLIC SCHOOL 


LESS THAN 14 PLAYERS

1 rack for all
       
       - All players play two games in a row, then come off the court.
       - On your next turn, play twice in a row again and come off when done.


       In other words, 

winning and non winning status does not apply


       FIRST GAMES OF THE DAY ONLY!
       At the end of the first game the winners remain on the court and separate.
       Non winners leave the court.


Regular games to 11 points.​


​When 6 players are waiting on each side we switch to 4 off - 4 on.


14 PLAYERS OR MORE

1 rack for Winners and 1 rack for Non-Winners

       - All 4 players come off the court, 4 new players go on.

Game ends when one of the teams reaches 9 points.  
No 2 points difference.
