Member Etiquette: 
MAP is a shared experience for everyone to enjoy.

Each of us can make it better for all by being considerate of all players regardless of the type.

Our members and volunteers all accept the responsibility to treat one another with Caring, Honesty and Respect.

We ask that members respect all posted guidelines in specific program areas.

Please feel free to speak with a MAP Director if there are any questions.
